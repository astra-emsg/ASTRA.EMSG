If you do a change on the Benchmarkauswertung*.rdlc please do it also on the Benchmarkauswertung*OhneGruppe.rdlc!

Create Benchmarkauswertung*.OhneGruppe.rdlc from Benchmarkauswertung*.rdlc:
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

1) Overwrite Benchmarkauswertung*.OhneGruppe.rdlc with Benchmarkauswertung*.rdlc
2) Open in Designer
3) Click on the "Eigenschaften der Gruppe" Cell. (Remember the Width of the Cell)
4) Click on the Second Cell in the First Row
5) Right Click on the DesignerHeader of the "Eigenschaften der Gruppe" Column (Delete Columns..)
6) Click on the First Subreport
7) Decrease the Width with the remembered Width
